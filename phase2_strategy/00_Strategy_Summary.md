# Strategy Summary - KBFA Homepage

> Date: 2026-04-03
> Author: Strategist reference alignment
> Status: Draft

## Strategic Conclusion

KBFA website V1 should win not by technical complexity, but by combining:

- institutional trust
- visible proof of activity
- simple action paths for inquiry and sponsorship

This makes the site feel more credible than a generic culture-promotion page and more actionable than a static brochure. `[Phase0-리서치][Phase1-사용자][추론]`

## Competitive Position

The site should position KBFA as:

- more official than tourism-style landing pages
- more approachable than rigid institutional sites
- more transparent than generic donation pages

## Phase 3 BA Writer Strategy Guidance

### Required reflections in BA documents

| Differentiation Point | Related FR Area | BA / UX Direction | Priority |
|----------------------|-----------------|-------------------|----------|
| Trust-first presentation | About, Homepage, Contact | Prioritize mission clarity, institutional tone, and clean hierarchy | Must |

### Design differentiation notes

- Signature experience:
  - calm institutional homepage
  - proof-oriented activity storytelling
  - trustworthy sponsorship journey
- Avoid:
  - overly decorative tourism look
  - crowded government-portal look
  - donation flow without fund-usage context
- Brand and UX keywords:
  - Trustworthy
  - Warm
  - Diplomatic

## Positioning Risks

| Risk | Impact | Response |
|------|--------|----------|
| Too much cultural warmth, not enough institutional trust | Weakens credibility for partners | Use disciplined structure and logo-led brand restraint |
| Too much institutional tone, not enough warmth | Loses emotional accessibility | Keep sponsorship and activity sections human and story-based |
| Sponsorship flow becomes too operationally heavy for V1 | Delays launch | Keep manual verification model for V1 |
