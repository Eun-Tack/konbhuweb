# Competitive Positioning - KBFA Homepage

> Date: 2026-04-03
> Status: Draft

## Positioning Statement

KBFA website V1 should be positioned as the official Korea-Bhutan friendship gateway that combines institutional trust, cultural warmth, and transparent support participation. `[Phase0-리서치][Phase1-사용자][추론]`

## Positioning Map

### Axis A

- Low trust presentation -> High trust presentation

### Axis B

- Static information only -> Actionable engagement and sponsorship

KBFA target zone:

- high trust presentation
- medium to high actionable engagement

## Message Hierarchy

### Primary message

KBFA is a trusted bridge between Korea and Bhutan.

### Secondary messages

- KBFA has real activity history and partnership credibility
- KBFA offers practical routes for inquiry and sponsorship
- KBFA operates across culture, exchange, tourism, and collaborative projects

## Risk and Counter-Positioning

| Risk | Counter-Positioning |
|------|---------------------|
| Looking like a tourism campaign microsite | Emphasize association identity, history, and partners |
| Looking like a rigid static institution | Use warm imagery, approachable copy, and activity storytelling |
| Looking like a generic donation site | Keep project purpose and fund usage visible |

## Strategic Fit for BA and Design

- Navigation should stay conservative and clear
- Homepage should prioritize trust before action
- Sponsorship should be visible but not overpower the association identity
- Content structure should support public-proof storytelling
