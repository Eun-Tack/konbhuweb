# Differentiation Strategy - KBFA Homepage

> Date: 2026-04-03
> Status: Draft

## Axis 1: UX Differentiation

| Differentiation Point | Current Common Pattern | KBFA Target | User Impact | Difficulty |
|-----------------------|------------------------|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| Clear trust-first homepage | Many small organizations have vague or cluttered introductions | Immediate understanding of who KBFA is and why it matters | High | Low |
| Sponsorship split by intent | Donation pages often mix all giving paths together | Separate project sponsorship and one-time sponsorship clearly | High | Medium |
| Contact clarity | Contacts are often buried or weakly structured | Strong contact page plus inquiry form | Medium | Low |

## Axis 1-B: Design and Brand Differentiation

| Design Point | Common Competitive Impression | KBFA Target Impression | Operational Effect | Difficulty |
|--------------|-------------------------------|------------------------|-------------------|-----------|
| Institutional warmth | Generic travel or brochure aesthetics | Diplomatic, warm, official | Builds trust without stiffness | Medium |
| Proof-oriented storytelling | Activities hidden in PDFs or lists | Timeline and highlight storytelling | Improves credibility scanability | Medium |
| Brand-led restraint | Overuse of decorative motifs | Use logo colors and symbolism with control | Prevents visual fatigue | Low |

## Axis 2: Feature Depth

| Feature Area | Typical Level | KBFA Goal | Strategic Value |
|-------------|--------------|-----------|----------------|
| Activity archive | Simple gallery or news list | Highlighted activity cards plus richer detail pages | High |
| Sponsorship | Static account info or generic donation page | Project detail, purpose, usage, target, and donor flow | High |
| Content operations | Hardcoded pages | Markdown-driven managed content | High |

## Priority Focus

KBFA should focus on these three differentiators:

1. Trust-first institutional identity
2. Proof-rich activities and history
3. Transparent sponsorship journeys

## Why Not More

This is a first-release association website, not a full platform product. Over-expanding differentiation themes would increase noise and delay implementation. `[추론]`
