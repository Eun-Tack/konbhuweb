# Brand Direction

> Author: Codex
> Date: 2026-04-02

## Visual Direction

The site should feel:

- official
- warm
- cross-cultural
- trustworthy
- story-rich

## Brand Cues from the Logo

- Deep blue: trust, continuity, diplomacy
- Red: energy, visibility, cultural exchange
- White dragon motif: symbolic distinctiveness and heritage
- Circular seal composition: institutional authority

## Design Guidance

- Avoid a generic tourism brochure look
- Avoid overly corporate monochrome styling that hides the cultural identity
- Use spacious layouts, large section headers, and documentary-style images
- Let red and blue accents lead; use gold only as a restrained secondary accent if needed
- Consider a soft paper, ink, or archival texture instead of flat white everywhere

## Typography Direction

- Korean: modern but dignified sans serif or serif-sans pairing
- English: supporting role only, not visually dominant
- Heading system should balance institutional clarity with cultural warmth

## Interaction Direction

- Calm reveal animations
- Timeline storytelling
- Card-based event highlights
- Accessible navigation and readable text blocks

## Sample Reuse Guidance

Reuse:

- section sequence
- basic hero and content rhythm
- contact/footer framing

Do not reuse without review:

- existing Korean copy
- oversized dependency set
- image choices unrelated to the association
- any section labels not backed by the PPT or approved content
