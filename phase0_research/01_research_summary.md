## Project Goal

Create an official homepage for the Korea Bhutan Friendship Association that introduces the association clearly, demonstrates activity credibility, and gives visitors a simple path to connect or collaborate. `[ppt][logo][inference]`

## Key Insights

### 1. Credibility matters as much as aesthetics

The PPT is heavily portfolio-driven, with repeated references to tourism promotion, culture events, MOU activity, public-sector connections, and international collaboration. The site therefore needs visible proof sections, not only a high-level mission statement. `[ppt]`

### 2. The provided sample is structurally helpful

The sample already uses a homepage-friendly section model: hero, about, history, business, news, network, and contact. This is a good information architecture baseline for an association site. `[sample]`

### 3. Visual tone should be more official than the sample

The logo has stronger institutional character than the sample's warm tourism look. The real site should feel more diplomatic, trustworthy, and archival, while still keeping Bhutan-friendly warmth. `[logo][sample][inference]`

### 4. Timeline content is a major asset

The PPT references activities across 2017 and 2023-2026, including tourism promotion, forums, MOU records, cultural events, charity events, and exhibitions. This gives the homepage a strong basis for a timeline and portfolio section. `[ppt]`

## Risks to Manage Early

- Dates and event names from the PPT should be rechecked with the association before public launch. `[ppt][inference]`
- Contact channels are not yet confirmed from the supplied resources. `[inference]`
- The sample code may slow us down if we inherit too many unnecessary UI dependencies. `[sample][inference]`

## Recommended Positioning

Use the homepage as a public trust and relationship-building platform:

- who the association is
- why Korea-Bhutan friendship matters
- what the association has done
- who it works with
- how to contact or collaborate

That positioning fits the source materials better than a tourism-only or campaign-only landing page. `[ppt][logo][inference]`
