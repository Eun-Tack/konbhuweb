## Flexible Content Rule

For implementation, these activity cards or timeline items should be built so the team can:

- change title
- change year
- change summary
- change image
- reorder items
- hide items without redesign

Images from the supplied PPT may be reused where appropriate, subject to final owner approval and export quality checks.

## Recommended Display Pattern

- Homepage:
  - show 3 to 5 featured highlights
- About or Activities detail page:
  - show full timeline and richer archive
