# Sitemap and Information Architecture

> Author: Codex
> Date: 2026-04-02

## Global Structure

The site should support two language trees:

- Korean primary
- English parallel

## Top Navigation

1. About
2. Major Activities
3. Partners
4. News
5. Sponsorship
6. Contact

## Recommended Route Structure

- `/about`

- `/activities/[slug]`
- `/partners`
- `/news`

- `/sponsorship`

## Homepage Section Order

1. Hero
2. KBFA introduction
3. History snapshot
4. Featured activities
5. Partner and network highlight
6. Sponsorship CTA block
7. Latest news
8. Contact and directions teaser

## About Page Structure

- mission
- organization overview
- history
- representative message if approved
- directions

## Activities Page Structure

- featured activity cards
- timeline archive
- activity detail links

## Sponsorship Page Structure

- sponsorship introduction
- choose between project sponsorship and one-time sponsorship
- receipt guidance
- FAQ or process summary

## Contact Page Structure

- inquiry form
- email
- phone
- address
- directions or map

## Popup Placement

Popup support should be globally available and controlled by content:

- homepage popup
- campaign popup
- important notice popup

Popups should allow expiration dates and language targeting.
