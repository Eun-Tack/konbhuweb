# Vision and Scope - KBFA Homepage

> Date: 2026-04-03
> Status: Draft

## Vision

Build the official KBFA website as a bilingual public trust platform that introduces the association, demonstrates proven activities, supports partnership outreach, and enables practical sponsorship participation.

## Product Value

- strengthen official association identity
- surface activity credibility and continuity
- create a reliable inquiry route
- enable transparent support participation

## In Scope

- Korean main site and English parallel pages
- About including history
- Major Activities and activity detail pages
- Partners
- News and announcements
- Sponsorship hub, project sponsorship, and one-time sponsorship
- Contact page with inquiry form
- Popup support
- Markdown-driven content operations

## Out of Scope

- Full CMS in V1
- Full automated bank reconciliation in V1
- Advanced internal admin dashboard in V1

## Success Conditions

- visitors understand KBFA quickly
- credibility is visible through activity proof
- contact and sponsorship routes are easy to find
- content updates remain owner-manageable
